What is this site?

The site will primarily focus on displaying comic books piece by piece, allowing the illustrations to flow at the users preferred speed. The soundtracks will be programmed to progress and change as the story goes on. Users may select the official soundtrack, or choose from an array of audio submitted by other users. This benefits everyone by providing more options for readers and offers exposure for audio engineers.

How will accounts work?

There will be individual accounts for people to sign into to save their favorite books and/or audio projects. Users may follow popular authors and see what's new, and allow popular contributors to provide suggestions for other books and soundtracks.

Future Features

Future features may include soundtracks by famed composers included with a premium subscription, as well as illustrations by popular users for books that leave more to the imagination than desired.

What formats can I upload?

You can upload comics in various formats including JPEG, PNG, and PDF.

What type of tools will the website have?

The site will have tools for authors that help split apart the individual scenes of the comic books, such as a snipping/cropping tool integrated inside the editor.
